Market Structure Analysis

Cardano has undergone a significant shift in momentum over the past week. After surging to over $1.10 in a powerful rally, ADA has reversed sharply, breaking through multiple support levels and now challenging the critical $0.75-$0.80 support zone. This correction appears to be part of a broader crypto market pullback.

The technical indicators confirm bearish control in the immediate term, with RSI readings near oversold territory across multiple timeframes. The MACD shows negative momentum on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts, though the 15-minute chart shows early signs of a potential momentum shift. Particularly notable is the extremely high ADX reading on the 15-minute chart (59.43), indicating that the current downtrend has exceptional strength.

Price is trading near the lower Bollinger Band on multiple timeframes, suggesting extreme oversold conditions that may soon lead to a technical bounce. However, the persistence of selling pressure and the magnitude of the recent decline suggest caution before assuming a reversal is imminent.

Conclusion

Given today's high-impact economic events and the current market volatility, a cautious approach is warranted. The most prudent strategy would be to wait for signs of stabilization before establishing significant positions, while carefully managing risk exposure in this challenging market environment.
