Market Structure Analysis

Cardano has experienced significant price fluctuation driven by President Trump's announcement regarding a US crypto reserve. The initial surge demonstrated strong positive market reaction, but the subsequent decline highlights the speculative nature of the move.

The technical structure shows ADA currently trading in a consolidation phase after this volatility. Price is attempting to establish support in the $0.90-$0.95 zone, which represents a significant retracement from the recent highs but still maintains higher lows compared to February's levels.

The technical indicators predominantly show neutral readings across timeframes, with some bullish bias on the 4-hour chart. The ADX readings suggest a moderately developing trend, particularly on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts, indicating potential for a directional move following this consolidation phase.

Conclusion

Cardano is currently in a consolidation phase following significant news-driven volatility. The surge after Trump's crypto reserve announcement and subsequent pullback highlight both the potential for policy-driven gains and the speculative nature of such moves.

The key level to watch is $0.95 for bulls, as a decisive break above this resistance would signal potential continuation toward the $1.00-$1.05 region. For bears, the $0.90 support zone is critical, as a breakdown would suggest further weakness toward $0.85 or below.

Given the mixed technical signals and the impact of recent news, a balanced approach is warranted. The most prudent strategy would be to wait for clear breakout or breakdown signals before establishing significant positions, while remaining vigilant for additional policy announcements that could dramatically shift sentiment in this news-sensitive market.
