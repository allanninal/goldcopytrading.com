Trading Strategy

Buy Strategy

Entry Conditions

Breakout Setup:
  • Price breaks above $0.65 resistance level
  • RSI moves above 55 on 1H and 4H charts
  • MACD confirms bullish crossover
  • Entry after confirmation with volume
Support Bounce Setup:
  • Price pulls back to test $0.63 support
  • RSI shows bullish divergence or above 40
  • MACD shows decreasing bearish momentum
  • Entry after support confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Below nearest swing low (~$0.60)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $0.68 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $0.72 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $0.75 (20% of position)

Sell/Short Strategy

Entry Conditions

Resistance Rejection Setup:
  • Price rejects at $0.65 resistance zone
  • RSI shows overbought conditions (>70)
  • Bearish engulfing or rejection candle
  • Entry after rejection confirmation
Breakdown Setup:
  • Price breaks below $0.63 support
  • Increased volume on breakdown
  • No bullish divergence present
  • Entry after breakdown confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Above nearest swing high (~$0.66)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $0.60 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $0.58 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $0.55 (20% of position)

Traders should focus on key levels ($0.63 support and $0.65 resistance) for high-probability entry opportunities while maintaining strict risk management.
