Cardano has been trading in a broader uptrend since early 2024, with the recent price action showing a significant spike following President Trump's crypto reserve announcement, followed by a sharp correction. The low ADX readings on the shorter timeframes confirm the lack of strong trend momentum at present, suggesting a period of consolidation before the next directional move.

Conclusion

Cardano (ADA) is currently in a consolidation phase following significant volatility triggered by President Trump's crypto reserve announcement. The cryptocurrency appears to be establishing support above the $0.930 level while facing resistance near $0.945-$0.955.

The key level to watch is $0.945 for bulls, as a decisive break above this resistance would signal potential continuation toward the psychologically important $1.000 level. For bears, the $0.930 support zone is critical, as a breakdown would suggest further weakness toward $0.910 or below.
