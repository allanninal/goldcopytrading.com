Market Structure Analysis

Bitcoin has experienced a sharp correction from near $100,000 to the $80,000 level. This represents a roughly 20% correction, which is not uncommon for Bitcoin even in bull markets. The price has found support around the $80,000 level and is now in a potential accumulation phase.

The longer-term uptrend remains intact despite this correction, with higher lows when viewing the weekly chart (not shown). The current price action suggests a potential bottoming process with early signs of bullish momentum returning.

Market Context

The current BTC/USD price action should be analyzed within these key market factors:

  • Significant structural change with a powerful rally from $80,000 to test $95,000
  • V-shaped recovery has invalidated the previous February downtrend
  • Price trading well above key moving averages across multiple timeframes
  • High ADX readings (47.84 on 1H) confirming strong trend momentum
  • Current consolidation suggesting potential continuation rather than reversal

Conclusion

BTC/USD has undergone a significant shift from bearish to bullish momentum, with a powerful rally that has changed the market structure. Despite the current -1.77% pullback on the 4-hour timeframe, the technical indicators suggest strong underlying momentum that could propel Bitcoin toward the psychologically important $100,000 level in the coming weeks.

The most prudent approach would be to look for entries during pullbacks to key support levels, particularly the $90,000 area, rather than chasing the current price action. For more aggressive traders, breakout entries above $95,000 could offer opportunities with tight stop losses.

The overall technical picture suggests maintaining a bullish bias as long as Bitcoin holds above key support levels, with careful attention to risk management given the potential for volatility around major psychological price levels.
