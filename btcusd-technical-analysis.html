Market Structure Analysis

Bitcoin has broken down from its multi-month uptrend, with a clear shift in market structure occurring in early March. The price has fallen below all significant moving averages, with the 50-day EMA now acting as strong overhead resistance. The violation of the important $86,000 support zone has triggered accelerated selling, with the price now searching for a bottom.

The technical structure is predominantly bearish, with Bitcoin trading below all major EMAs across multiple timeframes. The 20/50/200 EMA relationship is bearish on all observed timeframes, providing multiple layers of overhead resistance for any recovery attempts.

Technical indicators universally show bearish conditions, though multiple timeframes now display oversold readings. The RSI is approaching or in oversold territory across timeframes, particularly on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts, while the MFI on the 15-minute chart indicates extreme selling pressure. The MACD shows deeply negative momentum across timeframes, confirming the strong bearish bias currently in control.
