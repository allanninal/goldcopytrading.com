High-Impact Events Analysis

President Trump Speaks (02:10 UTC)

Given Trump's recent announcement about a US crypto reserve, any additional comments on cryptocurrency policy could significantly impact Bitcoin prices. Positive comments about Bitcoin's role in the reserve could trigger strong bullish momentum, while any clarification limiting Bitcoin's role could lead to profit-taking. Traders should be prepared for heightened volatility during and immediately after this speech.

ADP Non-Farm Employment Change (13:15 UTC)

This employment indicator is particularly important for Bitcoin as it influences broader market risk sentiment and dollar strength. The forecast shows a decrease to 141K from the previous 183K. A significantly lower number would suggest economic weakening, potentially accelerating Federal Reserve rate cuts, which would be bullish for Bitcoin. Conversely, a stronger-than-expected number could temporarily strengthen the dollar and pressure Bitcoin prices.

ISM Services PMI (15:00 UTC)

The services sector represents a major portion of the US economy, making this a crucial indicator. The forecast of 52.5 (vs previous 52.8) suggests slight slowing but still expansionary conditions. A reading below 50 would indicate contraction and could trigger risk-off sentiment initially, but might ultimately be positive for Bitcoin if it accelerates expectations of monetary easing. A stronger reading could temporarily pressure Bitcoin as it would support a stronger dollar.

Beige Book (19:00 UTC)

The Federal Reserve's Beige Book provides qualitative assessments of economic conditions across different districts. Markets will scrutinize the language for indicators of inflation trends and economic growth patterns. Any language suggesting persistent inflation could delay rate cuts and temporarily pressure Bitcoin, while indications of economic weakness could support Bitcoin prices by increasing the likelihood of monetary easing.
