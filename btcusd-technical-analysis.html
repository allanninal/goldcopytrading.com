Market Structure Analysis

Bitcoin has undergone a significant shift in structure over the past several days. After an impressive rally to test the $95,000 level, BTC has experienced a sharp correction, with the current price representing a pullback of approximately 12% from the recent high. This correction has broken several support levels and reversed the short-term bullish momentum.

The technical indicators universally confirm bearish control in the immediate timeframe, with RSI readings in oversold territory on the 15-minute and 1-hour charts. The MACD shows strong negative momentum across all timeframes, with the 4-hour chart displaying a recent bearish crossover that suggests potential for continued downside.

A noteworthy technical development is the ADX readings showing strong trend momentum, particularly on shorter timeframes. This indicates that the current correction has significant force behind it, though the oversold conditions may soon provide the basis for a technical bounce.
