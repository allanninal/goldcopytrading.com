Market Structure Analysis

SOL/USD has experienced a significant shift in market structure over the past week, from a bearish trend to a clearly bullish one. The price has broken above several key support levels, confirming a series of lower highs and lower lows across multiple timeframes.

This bullish transformation is evidenced by the price trading well above all major moving averages (20, 50, and 200 EMAs) on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts. The EMAs have aligned in a bullish configuration, with each acting as dynamic support during recent retracement attempts.

Volume analysis shows increased buying pressure, with volume spikes accompanying upward price movements. The technical indicators universally suggest that bullish momentum remains dominant, with the MACD showing strong positive readings and RSI values approaching but not yet reaching extreme overbought conditions on higher timeframes.
