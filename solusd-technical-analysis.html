Market Structure Analysis

SOL/USD has experienced a significant shift in market structure over the past week, from a bullish trend to a clearly bearish one. The price has broken below several key support levels, confirming a series of lower highs and lower lows across multiple timeframes.

This bearish transformation is evidenced by the price trading well below all major moving averages (20, 50, and 200 EMAs) on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts. The EMAs have aligned in a bearish configuration, with each acting as dynamic resistance during recent retracement attempts.

Volume analysis shows increased selling pressure, with volume spikes accompanying downward price movements. The technical indicators universally suggest that bearish momentum remains dominant, with the MACD showing strong negative readings and RSI values approaching but not yet reaching extreme oversold conditions on higher timeframes.
