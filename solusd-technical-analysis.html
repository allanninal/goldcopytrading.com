Trading Strategy

Buy Strategy

Entry Conditions

Breakout Setup:
  • Price breaks above and confirms $150 resistance
  • RSI stays above 50 on 1H and 4H charts
  • MACD confirms bullish crossover on 4H
  • Entry after confirmation with increased volume
Support Bounce Setup:
  • Price pulls back to test $140 support
  • RSI shows bullish divergence or stays above 40
  • MACD shows decreasing bearish momentum
  • Entry after support confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Below nearest swing low (~$135)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $155 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $170 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $185 (20% of position)

Sell/Short Strategy

Entry Conditions

Resistance Rejection Setup:
  • Price rejects at $150 resistance zone
  • RSI shows overbought conditions (>70)
  • Bearish engulfing or rejection candle
  • Entry after rejection confirmation
Breakdown Setup:
  • Price breaks below $140 support
  • Increased volume on breakdown
  • No bullish divergence present
  • Entry after breakdown confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Above nearest swing high (~$155)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $135 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $125 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $115 (20% of position)

Conclusion

Solana is currently in a critical phase after a significant downtrend followed by a recovery attempt. The asset is consolidating in the $140-150 range with mixed signals across timeframes. While the longer-term trend remains bearish, there are early signs of potential reversal. Traders should focus on key levels ($140 support and $150 resistance) for high-probability entry opportunities while maintaining strict risk management.
