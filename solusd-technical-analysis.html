Market Structure Analysis

SOL/USD has undergone a significant structural shift in recent days, with a powerful rally that has reversed the February downtrend. The charts show a sharp V-shaped recovery from lows around $135-$140, with Solana surging nearly 30% to test the $180 level before entering the current consolidation phase.

This dramatic move has altered the market structure from bearish to bullish, with price now trading well above key moving averages across multiple timeframes. The technical indicators universally suggest that the overall momentum remains bullish despite the current pullback, which appears to be a healthy consolidation after such a rapid rise.

The MACD readings on the 4-hour chart show positive momentum, with the histogram expanding in bullish territory. The ADX readings, particularly on the 1-hour chart (41.54), confirm that the recent upward move is backed by substantial strength rather than being merely a short-term fluctuation.
