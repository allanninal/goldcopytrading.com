Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment to start copy trading?

You can start copy trading with a very small initial deposit using cents accounts at LiteFinance. This allows you to participate with minimal risk while still copying all trades proportionally to verify strategies work for you.

How does the cents account work?

A cents account allows you to trade with micro lot sizes that are 1/100 of standard lots. This means your risk per trade is significantly reduced, making it perfect for beginners or those wanting to test strategies with minimal capital before scaling up.

Are there any fees for copy trading?

There are typically no upfront fees for copying trades - connection is usually free. Most traders charge a performance-based commission (typically 20-30%) only when your account makes a profit, ensuring their interests are aligned with yours. This structure means they're fully motivated to generate consistent profits for you.

How can I withdraw my profits?

You can withdraw your profits at any time through your broker's standard withdrawal process. Your funds remain in your own account at all times, and you maintain full control over deposits and withdrawals.

Can I modify the risk level when copying trades?

Yes, LiteFinance allows you to adjust the copying ratio. This means you can increase or decrease the position sizes relative to the trader's signals based on your personal risk tolerance.

How do I choose the right trader to copy?

When selecting traders to copy, consider their historical performance, drawdown levels, risk management approach, and consistency. Look for traders with at least 3-6 months of verifiable performance history, reasonable drawdown levels (typically under 30%), and a trading style that matches your risk tolerance and financial goals.
