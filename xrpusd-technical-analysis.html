Market Structure Analysis

XRP has undergone a significant shift in momentum over the past week. After surging to near $3.00 in a powerful rally, XRP has reversed sharply, breaking through multiple support levels and now challenging the critical $2.20-$2.30 support zone. This correction appears to be part of a broader crypto market pullback.

The technical indicators unanimously confirm strong bearish control in the immediate term, with RSI readings in or near oversold territory across multiple timeframes. The MACD shows negative momentum, with a recent bearish crossover on the 4-hour chart suggesting continued downside pressure. Particularly notable is the extremely high ADX reading on the 15-minute chart (54.60), indicating that the current downtrend has exceptional strength.

Price is trading near or below the lower Bollinger Band on multiple timeframes, suggesting extreme oversold conditions that may soon lead to a technical bounce. However, the persistence of selling pressure and the magnitude of the recent decline suggest caution before assuming a reversal is imminent.

Conclusion

XRP is currently experiencing a significant correction phase that has erased much of its recent gains. The technical picture is decidedly bearish across all timeframes, though extremely oversold conditions suggest a relief bounce may occur in the near term.

The $2.20-$2.30 support zone represents a crucial level that will likely determine the next significant directional move for XRP. A decisive break below this level could trigger further selling toward $2.00, while successful defense of this support could provide the foundation for a recovery attempt.

Given today's high-impact economic events and the current market volatility, a cautious approach is warranted. The most prudent strategy would be to wait for signs of stabilization before establishing significant positions, while carefully managing risk exposure in this challenging market environment.
