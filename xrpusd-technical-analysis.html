Trading Strategy

Buy Strategy

Entry Conditions

Breakout Setup:
  • Price breaks above $2.20-2.25 resistance zone
  • RSI moves above 55 on 1H and 4H charts
  • MACD crosses above signal line
  • Entry after confirmation with volume
Support Bounce Setup:
  • Price pulls back to test $2.15 support
  • RSI shows bullish divergence or above 40
  • MACD shows decreasing bearish momentum
  • Entry after support confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Below nearest swing low (~$2.05)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $2.30 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $2.45 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $2.60 (20% of position)

Sell/Short Strategy

Entry Conditions

Resistance Rejection Setup:
  • Price rejects at $2.20-2.25 resistance
  • RSI shows overbought conditions (>70)
  • Bearish engulfing or rejection candle
  • Entry after rejection confirmation
Breakdown Setup:
  • Price breaks below $2.15 support
  • Increased volume on breakdown
  • No bullish divergence present
  • Entry after breakdown confirmation

Risk Management:

  • Stop Loss: Above nearest swing high (~$2.25)
  • Position Sizing: Maximum 2% risk per trade
  • Take Profit Targets:
    • TP1: $2.05 (40% of position)
    • TP2: $2.00 (40% of position)
    • TP3: $1.90 (20% of position)

Additional Considerations

Bullish Scenarios

  • Daily close above $2.25 suggests potential reversal
  • Increasing volume on upward movements strengthens bull case
  • Regulatory clarity could be a positive catalyst

Bearish Scenarios

  • Failure to break $2.20 increases retest probability
  • Loss of $2.15 support could trigger renewed selling
  • Broader market weakness could amplify downside

Market Context

  • Ongoing legal and regulatory developments affecting Ripple
  • Correlation with Bitcoin and overall crypto market

  • Trading volumes and liquidity patterns

Conclusion

XRP is currently in a challenging phase after a significant downtrend followed by attempted consolidation. The asset is trading in the $2.15-2.20 range with mixed signals across timeframes. While the longer-term trend remains bearish, there are early signs of potential stabilization. Traders should focus on key levels ($2.15 support and $2.20-2.25 resistance) for high-probability entry opportunities while maintaining strict risk management.
