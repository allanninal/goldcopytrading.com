Market Structure Analysis

XRP has been in a period of consolidation following the volatile price action in early March, which was likely influenced by broader cryptocurrency market reactions to President Trump's crypto reserve announcement. The current market structure shows XRP trading below its medium-term moving averages but still maintaining its position above the critical 200 EMA on the daily chart, confirming that the longer-term uptrend remains intact despite the recent correction.

The technical indicators present a mixed picture across timeframes, typical of a consolidation phase. The RSI is in neutral territory on daily and 4-hour charts, neither confirming strong bearish momentum nor suggesting immediate reversal. The MACD shows minimal bullish momentum on the daily chart but remains slightly negative on the hourly timeframe, indicating the lack of strong directional bias in the near term.

The ADX reading of 23.8170 on the daily chart suggests a moderate trend strength, which aligns with the current consolidation pattern. The Bollinger Bands on lower timeframes show signs of contraction, often a precursor to a volatility expansion and potential breakout.
