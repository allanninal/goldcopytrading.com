Market Structure Analysis

EUR/USD has been trading in a broader range between $1.07 and $1.04 since early February, but has recently broken below this range, signaling a potential shift to a more bearish phase. The pair has formed a clear series of lower highs and lower lows across all timeframes, with the recent sharp decline breaking below previous support levels.

The technical indicators universally point to bearish momentum, with price trading below key moving averages and oscillators showing downward momentum. Of particular significance is the expansion of Bollinger Bands with price near the lower band, suggesting strong directional movement has taken hold.

The ADX readings, particularly on the 1-hour chart, indicate that the current bearish trend has significant strength, though the moderating readings on the 4-hour timeframe suggest we may soon see some consolidation before the next directional move.
