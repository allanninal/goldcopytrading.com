Risk Disclosure Statement

Trading in financial markets carries a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. Please ensure that you fully understand the risks involved.

1. General Risk Warning

Copy trading involves substantial risks, including the potential loss of your invested capital. You should not invest money that you cannot afford to lose. Before deciding to trade, you should carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience, and risk appetite.

2. Specific Risks of Copy Trading

  • Past performance is not indicative of future results
  • Trading results can vary significantly and no profit guarantees can be offered
  • Copy trading may result in losses equal to or greater than the risk settings you select
  • Delays in trade execution may impact results
  • Market conditions can change rapidly and affect trading performance
  • Leverage can work against you as well as for you

3. Market Risks

  • Gold (XAUUSD) prices can be highly volatile
  • Market gaps and slippage may occur, especially during high volatility periods
  • Economic and political events can significantly impact market movements
  • Trading during certain market conditions may be difficult or impossible

4. Technical Risks

  • Internet connectivity issues may affect trade execution
  • Platform or system failures could result in orders not being executed or delayed
  • Communication delays between copy trader and follower accounts may occur
  • Price feeds may be delayed or inaccurate

5. Account Risks

  • Different account sizes may experience different results
  • Broker spreads and commissions will affect overall performance
  • Account leverage settings may amplify both gains and losses
  • Stop-loss orders may not execute at desired levels during volatile markets

6. Strategy Specific Risks

Our copy trading strategy focuses exclusively on XAUUSD (Gold) trading, which carries its own specific risks:

  • Concentration risk from trading a single instrument
  • Gold prices can be affected by numerous factors including:
    • Global economic conditions
    • Currency fluctuations
    • Political events
    • Central bank policies

7. Regulatory and Legal Risks

  • Trading regulations may vary by jurisdiction
  • Tax implications may vary based on your location
  • Changes in regulations may affect trading conditions
  • Not all jurisdictions allow copy trading services

Risk Management Recommendations

  • Never invest more than you can afford to lose
  • Understand how copy trading works before starting
  • Monitor your account regularly
  • Consider starting with a smaller investment to test the service
  • Maintain a diversified investment portfolio
  • Be aware of all fees and costs involved
