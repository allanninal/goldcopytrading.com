Market Structure Analysis

Ethereum has experienced a dramatic shift in momentum over the past week. After showing signs of recovery with a move toward $2,500, ETH has reversed sharply, breaking through multiple support levels and is now challenging the critical $2,000 psychological level. This correction appears to be part of a broader crypto market pullback, with Bitcoin and other major cryptocurrencies also experiencing significant losses.

The technical indicators universally confirm strong bearish control in the immediate term, with RSI readings in or near oversold territory across all timeframes. The MACD shows powerful negative momentum, with no signs of imminent reversal yet. Particularly notable is the extremely high ADX reading on the 15-minute chart (56.97), indicating that the current downtrend has exceptional strength.

Price is trading at or below the lower Bollinger Band across all timeframes, suggesting extreme oversold conditions that may soon lead to a technical bounce. However, the persistence of selling pressure and the magnitude of the recent decline suggest caution before assuming a reversal is imminent.

Conclusion

Ethereum is currently experiencing a significant correction phase that has erased much of its recent gains. The technical picture is decidedly bearish across all timeframes, though extremely oversold conditions suggest a relief bounce may occur in the near term.

The psychological $2,000 level represents a crucial support zone that will likely determine the next significant directional move. A decisive break below this level could trigger further selling toward $1,800, while successful defense of this support could provide the foundation for a recovery attempt.

Given today's high-impact economic events and the current market volatility, a cautious approach is warranted. The most prudent strategy would be to wait for signs of stabilization before establishing significant positions, while carefully managing risk exposure in this challenging market environment.
