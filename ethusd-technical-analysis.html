Market Structure Analysis

Ethereum has been in a sustained downtrend since early 2025, with price repeatedly setting lower highs and lower lows. The recent price action shows ETH attempting to form a base around the $1927 level, with a potential double bottom formation developing on the 4-hour chart. The price is trading below all major moving averages across multiple timeframes, which is a bearish sign, but the shorter-term indicators are showing some signs of positive momentum building.

The technical structure shows ETH/USD struggling to break above the 20-period EMA on multiple timeframes, which has acted as dynamic resistance during this downtrend. The distance between the 50 and 200 EMAs on the daily chart continues to widen, indicating strengthening bearish momentum in the longer term.

The technical indicators show mixed signals across timeframes. The RSI is in bearish territory on the daily chart but neutral on the 4-hour chart, suggesting a potential short-term relief rally is possible. The MACD on the 1-hour chart shows positive momentum developing, while the MFI on the 15-minute chart indicates oversold conditions that could prompt a short-term bounce.
