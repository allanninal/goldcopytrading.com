Conclusion

Ethereum is currently navigating a crucial phase marked by consolidation and modestly bullish technicals. The cryptocurrency's ability to maintain price levels above $2,140 suggests underlying strength, though the market still requires a decisive break above $2,300 to confirm a more robust uptrend.

In the near term, traders should focus on how ETH responds at the identified resistance levels, particularly $2,250-$2,300. Favorable risk-reward opportunities exist on both pullbacks to support and potential breakouts above resistance, depending on your trading style and risk tolerance.

The broader crypto market sentiment and ETH's correlation with Bitcoin will continue to influence price action, so traders should monitor both BTC price movements and Ethereum-specific developments. As always, implementing strict risk management remains crucial, particularly given the asset's volatility.
