Market Structure Analysis

Ethereum has broken down from its multi-month uptrend, with a clear shift in market structure occurring in early March. The price has fallen below all significant moving averages, with the 50-day EMA now acting as strong overhead resistance. The violation of the important $2,000 support zone has triggered accelerated selling, with the price now searching for a bottom.

The technical structure is predominantly bearish, with Ethereum trading below all major EMAs across multiple timeframes. The 20/50/200 EMA relationship is bearish on all observed timeframes, providing multiple layers of overhead resistance for any recovery attempts.

Technical indicators universally show bearish conditions, though multiple timeframes now display oversold readings. The RSI is approaching or in oversold territory across timeframes, particularly on the 1-hour and 4-hour charts. The MACD shows negative momentum across timeframes, confirming the strong bearish bias currently in control.

    Weekend Risk: Reduce exposure or close positions before the weekend if trades are not showing clear profitability. Crypto markets trade 24/7, but weekend gaps in liquidity can create risk.

    Invalidation Planning: Prepare contingency plans if key support at $1,875-$1,900 breaks decisively or if resistance at $2,100 is breached.

Conclusion

Ethereum is displaying a definitively bearish technical picture as it approaches critical support at $1,900. The primary trend direction remains down with multiple timeframes showing bearish price structure.

The March 11-14 period presents multiple high-impact economic events that could significantly influence price direction, with the CPI release on March 12 potentially being the most market-moving. Trump's crypto speech on March 11 adds an additional layer of uncertainty.

Given the current technical setup, traders should maintain a primarily bearish bias while remaining vigilant for potential oversold bounces or range-bound conditions between $1,900-$2,000. The $1,875-$1,900 support zone will be critical to watch, as a breach could accelerate selling toward the $1,750-$1,800 region.
