Market Structure Analysis

Litecoin has been in a clear downtrend since early March, forming a sequence of lower highs and lower lows following the rejection at the $135 level. The price is currently testing a critical support zone around $87-$88, which if broken could accelerate selling pressure toward the $80-$82.50 area. The technical structure shows LTC/USD trading below all major moving averages across multiple timeframes, which is a negative sign for near-term momentum.

The technical indicators predominantly show deeply oversold conditions across all timeframes, with RSI under 30 on multiple timeframes. This suggests a potential short-term relief rally is possible, though the primary trend remains bearish. The MACD shows negative momentum across all timeframes, confirming the strong bearish bias currently in control.
